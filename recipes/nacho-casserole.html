Nacho Casserole

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large white onion (minced)
  • 2 large tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 (8 ounce) bag of tortilla chips
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 limes
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 2 cups of cheese (any melting cheese)
  • 2 sprigs of cilantro
  • 1 chopped jalapeno pepper (optional)
  • salt and pepper to taste
